On the next pages, you will be given some videos. Your task is to decide whether the video was manipulated or not. You may repeat the video, if you want to.

Additionally you need to provide the difficulty of the classification of the video e.g if you had no problems to detect that the video was manipulated select "Easy".

The reason you classified the video as manipulated or not-manipulated can be elaborated in an optional text-box. Eventhough the text-box may be optional, more information will help tremendously.
